BUILD-A-THON - A product Design Hackathon
  • 09:00 AM - 09:30 PM
  • 70 + participation

The very first design and prototyping hackathon of IITGN, enabling students to get a hands-on learning experience of brainstorming, designing and prototyping a product that could tackle a real-world problem under the budget of Rs10,000. Brainstormed three intriguing real-world challenges for the participants to test their creativity and problem-solving skills. An online session was conducted for the participants on “ PRODUCT DESIGN OVERVIEW” by Kaushik Bhaiya, an expert Product Manager at Wysa, for a deeper understanding of the product development process on 29th September. The rounds were evaluated by Prof Manasi Kanetkar.
