ABOUT US

The IR&P council will lookout for enhancing the industry-academia relations, bringing industrial projects, solving institute problems through IBPs, promoting entrepreneurial culture, spreading awareness about the upcoming industrial competitions, organizing events and hackathons, and documenting the ongoing and previous activities of the council.

  • 01 What about the Events?

    The IR&P Council Events Team is dedicated to providing industrial exposure and fostering entrepreneurship. This is achieved by organizing talks covering industrial research, job experiences, and insights across core industries, software, and AI domains also including topics from consulting to product management. Additionally, the team plans competitive events like hackathons, encouraging innovation within the industrial landscape. These initiatives aim to create opportunities for learning, networking, and hands-on experience.

  • 02 What are Industrial Projects ?

    IR&P council has one of its primary objectives to give students industrial exposure in the running semester. IR&P council contacts various industry giants and start-ups and brings the live industry problem statements for students to work. It gives students excellent exposure to working with industry professionals under the mentorship of IITGN faculties.

  • 03What are Institute Building Projects?

    Institute Building project(IBP) is an initiative of IIT GN. IR&P Council handles these projects. These projects aim to increase student participation in institute work and give them monetary incentives for their participation. Students build good connections with faculties and other students while working on these projects. These projects also provide them an exposure to solving real-life problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover quick answers to common queries. Here's a helpful guide to address your most frequently asked questions. Save time and find clarity.

  • Do I get paid if I take projects under IBP? How muh stipend can be expected?

    Yes, IBP projects are compensated at the standard ooCEO payment rate of Rs 150 per hour. To receive payment, individuals are required to fill out a monthly timecard, detailing the hours worked. Payments will be processed accordingly based on the documented hours of work.

  • What is the role of mentoring faculty and how is it helpful for me?

    The mentoring faculty primarily offers guidance on project direction and objectives, serving as project supervisors. While they don't provide physical resources or materials, they play a key role in advising and assisting mentees on the specific tasks and actions needed for the project's successful completion.

  • How are students selected for industrial projects?

    Students can express their interest in specific projects by filling out forms, and the first round of selection is based on resume, relevance of skills, and, most importantly, the student's motivation to contribute to the project. It is important to note that the selection process is undertaken by the company, not the council.

  • What are industrial projects, and how do they differ from regular coursework?

    Industrial projects involve applying academic knowledge to real-world challenges posed by industry partners. They provide hands-on experience beyond traditional classroom learning.

  • What kind of events does the IR&P Council organize?

    The council organizes a range of events, from organizing talks to hackathons, all of which are focused on improving industry-academia relations. For more information, you can visit the events page on our website.
